About Netra.AI

Vision, Precision,
and Purpose.

An open research initiative making clinical-grade eye disease screening accessible worldwide.

Netra.AI is a revolutionary AI-powered screening system engineered to assist clinicians in the early detection of common but vision-threatening eye diseases. Our deep learning pipeline analyses retinal fundus images with remarkable precision — rivalling specialist ophthalmologist performance on key benchmarks.

Our mission: make eye disease screening accessible, affordable, and accurate — stopping preventable blindness through early intervention, everywhere in the world.

"Early detection of eye disease can prevent up to 75% of vision loss cases worldwide."
Condition 01

Cataract

A clouding of the eye's natural lens, causing blurry vision, faded colours, and glare. The leading cause of vision loss globally — yet highly treatable with surgery when caught early.

Condition 02

Diabetic Retinopathy

A diabetes complication that damages retinal blood vessels. Without early detection it progresses to severe vision impairment. Routine screening is critical for all diabetic patients.

Condition 03

Glaucoma

Known as the "silent thief of sight," glaucoma damages the optic nerve with no early symptoms, causing irreversible peripheral vision loss. AI-based screening changes the prognosis.

Condition 04

Normal

A healthy retina with clear blood vessels, an intact optic disc, and an undamaged macula — the gold-standard baseline against which all conditions are assessed.

Key Features

Built for
Accuracy & Trust.

Everything a clinician or researcher needs for reliable, rapid retinal image analysis.

Deep Learning Engine

State-of-the-art CNN trained on thousands of labelled retinal images. Achieves 96.4% accuracy across four disease classes.

Real-Time Results

Full analysis completes in under five seconds. Interactive confidence charts and probability bars update live.

Full Transparency

Per-class precision, recall, and F1-scores are surfaced alongside every result. No black-box outputs.

Privacy by Design

Images are processed in-session only and never stored permanently. Your patient data stays yours.
